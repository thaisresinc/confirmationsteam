SCRIPT REFERENCE
Key Lines to Know
WARM OPENING
"Hi, thanks for calling RESINC Solar, you're speaking with [Your Name] — how can I help you today?"
GATHER DETAILS
"Let's get your details sorted first so I can build your profile properly and check your home for suitability — is that okay?"
ROOF CHECK
"I'm just going to quickly pull up your property now and take a look at the roof... Yeah, that actually looks like a really solid roof for solar."
BOOKING — SOLE OWNER
"So the next step is a solar assessment with one of our specialists. This will be done via Zoom and takes around 60 to 90 minutes. I've locked that in for you — you'll receive an email and SMS with all the details. Our confirmation team will call you the day before to make sure you're all set."
BOOKING — DUAL PARTY
"This appointment is booked for all decision-makers to attend together. The reason for that is because we go through everything — savings, system design, costs — and it's really important all decision-makers are there so you can make a fully informed joint decision. I've locked that in for both yourself and [Partner's Name]."
PRE-APPOINTMENT REQUIREMENTS
"Before the appointment, we just need a recent electricity bill showing usage, and a clear photo of your meter box. Would you be able to send those through before the appointment?"
CLOSE
"Perfect — that's everything from me. You're all set in the system now. We're looking forward to helping you get your electricity costs down. Thanks again for your time — have a great day!"
