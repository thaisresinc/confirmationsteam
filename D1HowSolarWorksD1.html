🌙 At night
No solar generation. Home draws from the grid as normal — or from a battery if installed. This is why battery storage adds significant value.
💰 Feed-in tariff
When solar produces more than the home uses, the excess is exported to the grid. The energy retailer pays a small rate per kWh for that export.

An automatic discount knocked straight off the invoice. No paperwork, no claiming — it just reduces the price. The exact amount depends on system size and location.

Team reference onlyDo not quote this figure directly to customers. The exact saving depends on location, system size and personal situation — that's exactly what the specialist assessment is there to maximise.
STATE GOVERNMENT · BATTERY REBATE
~30% off
$7.2 Billion Battery Program
Takes around 30% off the cost of a home battery. Steps down every six months — the longer someone waits, the less they get. Can only ever be claimed once per property.

ON THE PHONE
How to explain this simply
☀️
HOW IT WORKS
"Panels on the roof generate power, inverter converts it, your home uses it first — anything left over goes back to the grid."
Simple, accurate, easy to follow. Never go deeper than this unless they specifically ask.
💰
THE STC SUBSIDY
"The government subsidises it — most homeowners in your situation get a meaningful amount knocked straight off the price. No paperwork, it's automatic — the specialist will work out exactly what you're entitled to."
Lead with the benefit. The acronym STC means nothing to a customer — the dollar amount does.
⏱
THE BATTERY REBATE — URGENCY
"The battery rebate steps down every six months. It can only ever be claimed once per property — so the timing genuinely matters here."
This is real urgency, not manufactured pressure. Use it — but only because it's true.
