FINAL NOTE — FOCUS ON WHAT YOU CONTROL
The ACE Framework
A
A — ACTIVITY
Dial more. Talk more. Book more.
Volume is the foundation of everything. You can't control who picks up. You can control how many times you dial. More dials = more opportunities = more results.
C
C — CONVICTION
Every call deserves your best energy.
Customers feel energy through the phone. A flat voice kills warm leads. Show up with the same conviction on call 40 as you did on call 1. They can hear it — and they respond to it.
E
E — EXECUTE THE SYSTEM
Don't reinvent the wheel.
Follow the process. Work the frameworks. Trust the results. The system exists because it works. Your job is to execute it consistently — not to improvise your way to a different outcome.
